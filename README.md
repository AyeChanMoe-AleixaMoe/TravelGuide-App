 # Travel Guide App

**Travel Guide App** is an all-encompassing travel guide app developed in Kotlin, designed to streamline every aspect of your travel experience. It helps you organize your trips, manage your budget, and keep your important documents safe.

## Core Features

- **Dynamic Itinerary Planning**: Easily add, view, and manage your travel plans in a dynamic list. Your itinerary updates in real-time as you add new items.
- **Secure Document Storage**: Keep your passport, visas, and other important documents safe with PIN protection. 
- **Budget Tracking**: Monitor your expenses and stay on top of your budget with a clear and concise overview.
- **Quick Actions**: Access your flights, accommodations, and activities with a single tap.
- **Today's Schedule**: Get a quick glance at your daily schedule to stay organized on the go.

## Technologies Used

- **Jetpack Compose**: For building the app's modern and responsive UI.
- **MVVM Architecture**: For a clean, scalable, and maintainable codebase.
- **Coil**: For efficient image loading.
- **Room**: For local data storage.
- **Retrofit**: For network operations.
- **Coroutines**: For managing background threads and asynchronous operations.

## Getting Started

To get started with the app, you'll need:

- **Android Studio**
- **Android SDK**
- An active **internet connection**
